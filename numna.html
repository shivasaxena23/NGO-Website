About Number Nagar

NumberNagar by Brainstars is a unique hands-on Math activity center for Primary and secondary students. It takes Math off the school chapters and connects it to the city our children live in so that mathematical concepts are no longer alien entities but an exciting daily experience.

NumberNagar adopts a Playful approach of learning math and children are engaged in multi-disciplinary activities that encourage them to explore and city around then and learn the concept by making connections to the world that make sense to them. It adopts self-paced, self-led and learner centric methods where learning is enjoyable. It’s connected to curriculum across IB, IGCSE, ICSE, CBSE, and the State Board.
