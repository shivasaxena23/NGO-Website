About the Program

Word world provides fun and exciting strategies that build and strengthen literacy skills. It provides systematic instruction to phonics, fosters reading activities, and encourages to use the word power in developing good writing skills. The children will learn fun comprehension skills that helps in better understanding of the text along with vocabulary and fluency.

Phonics

Phonics is the method of teaching the individual sounds of the letters. These skills are very important to build strong reading skills in children. One has to use this to decode the words by sounding them out or blend together to form a word thus building sound-spelling pattern. Children enjoy and play numerous word games and become strong and confident readers.

Reading

Read to your children and give them the gift of becoming voracious readers. Reading is a process of comprehending and understanding the reading material. Reading includes a procedure where the reader identifies the words, pronounce its sound and learn the meaning to make full sense of the reading material. The reader has to recognize words, join together to read sentences and understand to get the full meaning. The reading can be interesting when one comprehends the printed material. When children are exposed to the reading from very early age, they develop an interest in reading and may the habit of reading.

Writing

Writing is one of the important means of communication. In the present world, good writing is essential for the success in school or at work place. It is a combination of skills which is best taught by breaking down the process. The writing process involves a series of steps like pre-writing, drafting, revising, editing and publishing while producing a finished piece of writing. Good guidance and constructive feedback helps the students to improve their writing skills and progressively enjoy and master the writing skills.
